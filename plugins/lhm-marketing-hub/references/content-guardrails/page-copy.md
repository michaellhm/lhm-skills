# Content Guardrail — Page Copy

Loaded by the content-writer agent for marketing page copy (homepage, landing pages, feature pages, about pages, pricing pages).

## Required structure

- Hero section with clear value prop (5-second test)
- Primary CTA above the fold
- 3–5 supporting sections that handle objections, build trust, demonstrate value
- Secondary CTA in the middle of the page
- Final CTA section

## Voice and tone

- Conversion-focused but not pushy
- Lead with benefits, support with features
- Avoid generic phrases ("seamless integration", "robust solution", "industry-leading")

## CTA hierarchy

- Primary CTA: action-oriented verb + outcome
- Secondary CTA: lower commitment (learn more, see pricing)

## Trust signals

- Place near decision points (above CTAs, at section breaks)
- Specific over vague ("used by 4,200 dentists" beats "trusted by thousands")
