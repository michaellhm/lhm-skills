# Content Guardrail — Blog Post

Loaded by the content-writer agent for blog post content (1500–3000 words typical).

## Required structure

- H1 with primary keyword (front-loaded where natural)
- Intro that resolves the search intent in the first 100 words
- 3–5 H2 sections with descriptive subheads (no question-mark-only headings)
- Internal links to relevant service pages or related posts
- Closing CTA section directing the reader to the next action

## Voice and tone

- Match the client's Campaign Playbook voice
- Avoid "let's explore", "let's dive into", "in this guide we'll cover"
- Avoid ending paragraphs with vague emotional insights
- Active voice over passive
- Specific examples and concrete details

## SEO

- Primary keyword in H1, first 100 words, one H2, and the conclusion
- Secondary keywords distributed naturally
- Meta description 140–155 chars
- Internal links: at least 2 to existing client content
- External links: 1–2 to authoritative sources (avoid competitors)
