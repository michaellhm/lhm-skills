## Key Takeaways from This Example

### What Made This Yellow Zone

1. **Strong performance** - All campaigns under target CPA (58% average)
2. **Budget headroom** - Only using 70% of available budget
3. **Client capacity** - Explicitly mentioned ability to handle more bookings
4. **New opportunity** - Bulk billing campaign showing early promise

### Why These Actions Were Recommended

1. **Budget increases** - Immediate, low-risk way to get more conversions
2. **Keyword expansion** - Increase auction inventory with geographic focus
3. **Nurture new campaign** - Patient growth of promising new channel
4. **Deferred actions** - User wisely chose to scale gradually

### Yellow Zone Best Practices

- Scale incrementally (10-20% budget increases)
- Expect CPAs to rise slightly - that's okay if still under target
- Focus on geographic/service area relevance
- Protect messaging consistency across campaigns
- Monitor weekly during scaling period
- Confirm client capacity before scaling

### Why Not All 5 Actions?

The user made a smart choice to defer broader match and ad schedule changes:
- Simpler to attribute results with fewer variables
- Can test those in subsequent months
- Maintains control during initial scaling
- Demonstrates thoughtful, phased approach
