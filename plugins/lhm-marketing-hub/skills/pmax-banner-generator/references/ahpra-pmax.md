# AHPRA Compliance for PMax Banners

Quick reference for validating Performance Max banner copy against AHPRA advertising guidelines for Australian healthcare.

## Pre-Flight Checklist

Before generating any healthcare PMax asset, verify:

- [ ] No guaranteed outcomes
- [ ] No superlatives without proof
- [ ] No therapeutic claims
- [ ] No success rates or statistics
- [ ] No before/after promises or imagery
- [ ] No testimonials or implied reviews
- [ ] Credentials are accurate
- [ ] CTAs are consultation-focused
- [ ] No urgency/fear language

## Prohibited Patterns

### Guaranteed Outcomes

| Banned | Why | Compliant Alternative |
|--------|-----|----------------------|
| Guaranteed results | Outcomes vary | Effective treatment options |
| 100% satisfaction | Can't guarantee | Dedicated to your care |
| Pain-free guaranteed | Outcome promise | Pain management solutions |
| We guarantee to fix | Outcome + therapeutic | We aim to help manage |
| Promise you'll feel better | Outcome promise | Supporting your recovery |

**Detection:** Contains "guarant", "100%", "promise", "will fix"

### Superlatives Without Proof

| Banned | Why | Compliant Alternative |
|--------|-----|----------------------|
| Best physio in Sydney | Unsubstantiated | Experienced Sydney physio |
| #1 chiropractor | No ranking proof | Trusted chiropractic care |
| Leading podiatrist | Needs evidence | Professional podiatry |
| Top-rated optometrist | Source needed | Quality optometry care |
| Most experienced | Comparative claim | Extensive experience |

**Detection:** Contains "best", "#1", "leading", "top", "most", "premier"

### Therapeutic Claims

| Banned | Why | Compliant Alternative |
|--------|-----|----------------------|
| Cures back pain | Therapeutic claim | Back pain assessment & care |
| Eliminates headaches | Therapeutic claim | Headache management |
| Fixes your posture | Therapeutic outcome | Posture improvement program |
| Heals sports injuries | Therapeutic claim | Sports injury rehabilitation |
| Removes sciatica | Therapeutic claim | Sciatica treatment options |

**Detection:** Contains "cure", "eliminate", "fix", "heal", "remove", "eradicate"

### Success Rates

| Banned | Why | Compliant Alternative |
|--------|-----|----------------------|
| 95% of patients improve | Unverifiable | Many patients report improvement |
| 9 out of 10 recommend | Statistical claim | Highly regarded by patients |
| 85% success rate | Unverifiable | Evidence-based approach |
| 90% pain reduction | Outcome statistic | Aims to reduce discomfort |

**Detection:** Contains percentages, "X out of Y", specific timeframes

### Before/After Claims

| Banned | Why | Compliant Alternative |
|--------|-----|----------------------|
| See the transformation | Outcome promise | Learn about our approach |
| Before and after | Comparative outcome | Treatment information |
| Look at these results | Outcome showcase | Meet our team |
| Amazing improvements | Superlative outcome | Dedicated care |

**Detection:** Contains "before", "after", "transformation", "results"

### Urgency/Fear Language

| Banned | Why | Compliant Alternative |
|--------|-----|----------------------|
| Don't let it get worse | Fear-based | Book when you're ready |
| Act now before too late | Urgency pressure | Appointments available |
| Limited spots | Artificial urgency | Booking available |
| Ignoring could be dangerous | Fear-based | Professional assessment |

**Detection:** Contains "don't wait", "act now", "limited", "dangerous"

## Safe Patterns

### Credential Language

| Pattern | Example |
|---------|---------|

## Image Prompt Compliance

### Prohibited Image Concepts

- Before/after comparisons
- Exaggerated body transformations
- Pain expressions or distress
- Medical procedures in progress
- Text overlays or logos
- Celebrity lookalikes
- Unrealistic body proportions

### Safe Image Concepts

- Clinical environment (clean, professional)
- Practitioner with patient (consultation setting)
- Treatment room (equipment visible, neutral)
- Reception/booking area (welcoming)
- Lifestyle movement (gentle activity, no extremes)
- Natural lighting preferred
- Diverse representation encouraged

### Image Prompt Template

```
Scene: [Clinical/reception/lifestyle setting]
Subject: [Practitioner type] with [patient type]
Mood: [Professional, welcoming, calm]
Lighting: [Natural, soft, clinical]
Composition: [Medium shot, environmental, close-up]
Style: [Realistic, professional photography]

Negative prompt: no before/after, no text, no logos, no pain expressions, no extreme transformations, no medical procedures, no unrealistic proportions
```

## Industry-Specific Notes

### Physiotherapy
**Safe:** Assessment, treatment options, rehabilitation, mobility, strength, movement
**Avoid:** Cure, fix, heal, guaranteed pain relief, miracle recovery

### Chiropractic
**Safe:** Spinal care, adjustment, alignment assessment, musculoskeletal, posture
**Avoid:** Cure, subluxation healing, guaranteed results, structural correction

### Podiatry
**Safe:** Foot care, assessment, orthotics, gait analysis, footwear advice
**Avoid:** Cure foot pain, permanent fix, guaranteed outcomes

### Psychology
**Safe:** Counselling, support, mental health assessment, wellbeing, strategies
**Avoid:** Cure mental illness, guaranteed happiness, fix your problems

### Cosmetic/Aesthetic
**Safe:** Consultation, treatment options, personalised approach, aesthetic goals
**Avoid:** Guaranteed results, perfect outcomes, dramatic transformation

## Compliance Checking Process

1. **Scan** copy for red flag words
2. **Review** context of flagged words
3. **Verify** credentials are accurate
4. **Check** CTAs are consultation-focused
5. **Review** image prompts for prohibited concepts
6. **Document** any changes made

## Status Assignment

### COMPLIANT
- No prohibited patterns
- Credentials verifiable
- Evidence-based language
- Consultation-focused CTAs
- Safe image concepts

### REWRITTEN
- Original contained prohibited pattern
- Automatically replaced with compliant alternative
- Note original and reason in compliance report
