# AHPRA Compliance Rules for Ad Copy

Quick reference checklist for validating Google Ads copy against AHPRA advertising guidelines.

---

## Pre-Flight Checklist

Before publishing any healthcare ad, verify:

- [ ] No guaranteed outcomes
- [ ] No superlatives without proof
- [ ] No therapeutic claims
- [ ] No success rates or statistics
- [ ] No before/after promises
- [ ] Credentials are accurate
- [ ] CTA is consultation-focused
- [ ] No urgency/fear language

---

## Prohibited Patterns

### 1. Guaranteed Outcomes ❌

| Banned Phrase | Why | Compliant Alternative |
|--------------|-----|----------------------|
| "Guaranteed results" | Outcomes vary by patient | "Effective treatment options" |
| "100% satisfaction" | Can't guarantee experience | "Dedicated to your care" |
| "Pain-free guaranteed" | Outcome promise | "Pain management solutions" |
| "We guarantee to fix" | Outcome + therapeutic | "We aim to help manage" |
| "Promise you'll feel better" | Outcome promise | "Supporting your recovery" |

**Detection Pattern:** Contains "guarant", "100%", "promise", "will fix"

---

### 2. Superlatives Without Proof ❌

| Banned Phrase | Why | Compliant Alternative |
|--------------|-----|----------------------|
| "Best physio in Sydney" | Unsubstantiated claim | "Experienced Sydney physio team" |
| "#1 chiropractor" | No verifiable ranking | "Trusted chiropractic care" |
| "Leading podiatrist" | Needs evidence | "Professional podiatry services" |
| "Top-rated optometrist" | Rating source needed | "Quality optometry care" |
| "Most experienced" | Comparative claim | "Extensive experience" |

**Detection Pattern:** Contains "best", "#1", "leading", "top", "most", "premier"

---

### 3. Therapeutic Claims ❌

| Banned Phrase | Why | Compliant Alternative |
|--------------|-----|----------------------|
| "Cures back pain" | Therapeutic claim | "Back pain assessment & care" |
| "Eliminates headaches" | Therapeutic claim | "Headache management options" |
| "Fixes your posture" | Therapeutic outcome | "Posture improvement program" |
| "Heals sports injuries" | Therapeutic claim | "Sports injury rehabilitation" |
| "Removes sciatica" | Therapeutic claim | "Sciatica treatment options" |

**Detection Pattern:** Contains "cure", "eliminate", "fix", "heal", "remove", "eradicate"

---

### 4. Success Rate Claims ❌

| Banned Phrase | Why | Compliant Alternative |
|--------------|-----|----------------------|
| "95% of patients improve" | Unverifiable statistic | "Many patients report improvement" |
| "9 out of 10 recommend" | Statistical claim | "Highly regarded by patients" |
| "85% success rate" | Unverifiable | "Evidence-based approach" |
| "90% pain reduction" | Outcome statistic | "Aims to reduce discomfort" |
| "Most patients better in 2 weeks" | Time-based claim | "Individual treatment plans" |

**Detection Pattern:** Contains percentages, "X out of Y", "most patients", specific timeframes

---

### 5. Before/After Promises ❌

| Banned Phrase | Why | Compliant Alternative |
|--------------|-----|----------------------|
| "See the transformation" | Outcome promise | "Learn about our approach" |
| "Before and after" | Comparative outcome | "Treatment information" |
| "Look at these results" | Outcome showcase | "Meet our team" |
| "Amazing improvements" | Superlative outcome | "Dedicated care" |

**Detection Pattern:** Contains "before", "after", "transformation", "results", "improvement"

---

### 6. Urgency/Fear Language ❌

| Banned Phrase | Why | Compliant Alternative |
|--------------|-----|----------------------|
| "Don't let it get worse" | Fear-based | "Book when you're ready" |
| "Act now before too late" | Urgency pressure | "Appointments available" |
| "Limited spots" | Artificial urgency | "Booking available" |
| "Ignoring could be dangerous" | Fear-based | "Professional assessment" |

**Detection Pattern:** Contains "don't wait", "act now", "limited", "before it's too late", "dangerous"

## Safe Patterns

### Credential Language ✅

| Pattern | Example |
|---------|---------|
| Registration | "AHPRA registered practitioners" |
| Qualifications | "University qualified team" |
| Experience | "15 years of experience" |
| Membership | "APA member" |
| Specialisation | "Specialising in sports injuries" |

---

### Evidence-Based Language ✅

| Pattern | Example |
|---------|---------|
| May help | "May help manage your symptoms" |
| Aims to | "Aims to improve mobility" |
| Supports | "Supports your recovery journey" |
| Options | "Treatment options available" |
| Assessment | "Professional assessment" |

## Compliance Checking Process

### Step 1: Scan for Banned Words

Check copy for these red flag words:

```
guarantee, guaranteed, guarantees
best, #1, leading, top, premier, most
cure, cures, eliminate, fix, heal, remove
% (percentages)
before, after, transformation, results
don't wait, act now, limited, dangerous
```

### Step 2: Review Context

If red flag word found, check context:
- Is it a banned pattern? → Flag as NON-COMPLIANT
- Is it borderline? → Flag as REVIEW REQUIRED
- Is it actually fine? → Mark COMPLIANT

### Step 3: Verify Credentials

If credentials claimed, verify:
- AHPRA registration verifiable?
- Years experience accurate?
- Qualifications claimed are held?

### Step 4: Check CTAs

Ensure CTAs are:
- Consultation-focused (not outcome-focused)
- Not creating false urgency
- Not fear-based

---

## Status Assignment Guide

### Assign ✅ COMPLIANT When:

- No prohibited patterns detected
- Credentials are verifiable
- Language is evidence-based
- CTAs are consultation-focused

### Assign ⚠️ REVIEW REQUIRED When:

- Borderline phrasing detected
- Context is ambiguous
- Regional variation possible
- New pattern not in guidelines

### Assign ❌ NON-COMPLIANT When:

- Clear prohibited pattern used
- Guaranteed outcome claimed
- Superlative without evidence
- Therapeutic claim made
- Success rate stated

## Healthcare Industry Specifics

### Physiotherapy
**Safe:** Assessment, treatment options, rehabilitation, mobility, strength
**Avoid:** Cure, fix, heal, guaranteed pain relief

### Chiropractic
**Safe:** Spinal care, adjustment, alignment assessment, musculoskeletal
**Avoid:** Cure, subluxation healing, guaranteed results

### Podiatry
**Safe:** Foot care, assessment, orthotics, gait analysis
**Avoid:** Cure foot pain, guaranteed results

### Optometry
**Safe:** Eye exam, vision assessment, eye health, prescription
**Avoid:** Perfect vision, cure eye problems

### Naturopathy
**Safe:** Natural health support, wellness consultation, holistic assessment
**Avoid:** Cure, treat disease, guaranteed outcomes

## Quick Reference Card

**Always Check For:**
1. Guaranteed → Remove
2. Best/Top/#1 → Remove or substantiate
3. Cures/Fixes/Heals → Change to "may help"
4. Percentages/Statistics → Remove
5. Before/After → Remove
6. Don't Wait/Act Now → Change to "when ready"

**Always Include:**
1. AHPRA registration (if claiming)
2. Consultation-focused CTA
3. Evidence-based language
4. Verifiable credentials only
