# RSA Structure Template

Reference guide for Responsive Search Ad structure, character limits, and best practices.

---

## RSA Specifications

### Headlines

| Requirement | Specification |
|-------------|---------------|
| **Quantity** | 15 headlines (minimum 3, max 15) |
| **Character Limit** | 30 characters per headline |
| **Display** | Google shows 2-3 headlines at a time |
| **Separator** | Headlines separated by " | " or " - " |

### Descriptions

| Requirement | Specification |
|-------------|---------------|
| **Quantity** | 4 descriptions (minimum 2, max 4) |
| **Character Limit** | 90 characters per description |
| **Display** | Google shows 1-2 descriptions at a time |
| **Separator** | Descriptions shown as separate sentences |

## Headline Categories (15 Total)

### Category 1: Keyword Headlines (3-4)

**Purpose:** Include target keywords for relevance and Quality Score

**Template:**
```
[Primary Keyword] [Location]
[Service Type] [Location]
[Location] [Primary Keyword]
[Adjective] [Primary Keyword]
```

**Examples (Physiotherapy):**
- `Physio Gold Coast` (16 chars)
- `Sports Physiotherapy` (20 chars)
- `Gold Coast Physio` (17 chars)
- `Local Physiotherapy` (19 chars)

**Character counting tips:**
- Keep location short (GC instead of Gold Coast if needed)
- Use common abbreviations (Physio, Chiro)

---

### Category 2: Benefit Headlines (2-3)

**Purpose:** Communicate patient benefits (AHPRA-compliant)

**Template:**
```
[Benefit Statement] (compliant)
[Outcome Focus] (careful wording)
[Patient Value]
```

**AHPRA-Compliant Examples:**
- `Effective Pain Management` (24 chars)
- `Supporting Your Recovery` (24 chars)
- `Back to Active Living` (21 chars)
- `Improving Your Mobility` (23 chars)

**Avoid:**
- ❌ "Pain-Free Guaranteed"
- ❌ "Cures Back Pain"

---

### Category 3: Credential Headlines (2-3)

**Purpose:** Build trust through qualifications

**Template:**
```
[Years] Years Experience
University Qualified [Profession]
[Association] Member
```

**Examples:**
- `15 Years Experience` (19 chars)
- `University Qualified` (20 chars)
- `Experienced Physios` (19 chars)
- `APA Member Clinic` (17 chars)

> Never use "AHPRA registered", "specialist", or "expert" in headlines.

---

### Category 4: CTA Headlines (2-3)

**Purpose:** Drive action (consultation-focused)

**Template:**
```
Book [Action] Today
[Action] Your Appointment
Get Your [Assessment Type]
Contact Us [Timeframe]
```

**AHPRA-Compliant Examples:**
- `Book Your Assessment` (20 chars)
- `Schedule Consultation` (21 chars)
- `Call Our Team Today` (19 chars)
- `Book Online Now` (15 chars)

**Avoid:**
- ❌ "Get Pain Relief Now"
- ❌ "Don't Wait - Book Now"

---

### Category 5: Location/Convenience Headlines (2-3)

**Purpose:** Emphasise accessibility and local presence

**Template:**
```
[Suburb] Location
Convenient [Feature]
[Accessibility] [Location]
Near [Landmark/Area]
```

**Examples:**
- `Surfers Paradise Clinic` (23 chars)
- `Free Onsite Parking` (19 chars)
- `Open 7 Days A Week` (18 chars)
- `Near Pacific Fair` (17 chars)
- `Easy CBD Access` (15 chars)

---

### Category 6: Differentiator Headlines (2-3)

**Purpose:** Highlight unique aspects of the practice

**Template:**
```
[Unique Feature]
[Area Of Focus] Care
[Patient Type] Care
[Approach] Based Care
```

**Examples:**
- `Sports Injury Care` (18 chars)
- `Work Injury Care` (16 chars)
- `Same Day Appointments` (21 chars)
- `Evidence-Based Care` (19 chars)
- `Personalised Treatment` (22 chars)

> Avoid "specialist" / "expert" wording — describe the area of focus as "care" instead.

## Description Structure (4 Total)

### Description 1: Core Service + Benefit

All descriptions are written in **Title Case** (every major word capitalised).

**Purpose:** Primary service description with patient benefit

**Template:**
```
[Core Service Description]. [Key Benefit Statement]. [Optional CTA].
```

**Example:**
```
Physiotherapy Services On The Gold Coast. Supporting Your Recovery Journey. Book Today.
```
(86 chars ✅)

---

### Description 2: Credentials + Trust

**Purpose:** Establish credibility through qualifications and experience

**Template:**
```
[Credential statement]. [Trust element]. [Differentiator].
```

**Example:**
```
University Qualified Physios With 15+ Years Experience. Trusted By Local Athletes.
```
(81 chars ✅)

---

### Description 3: CTA + Value

**Purpose:** Drive action with value proposition

**Template:**
```
[CTA]. [Value proposition]. [Supporting statement].
```

**Example:**
```
Book Your Assessment Today. Personalised Treatment Plans Tailored To Your Needs.
```
(80 chars ✅)

---

### Description 4: Differentiator + Secondary Benefit

**Purpose:** Reinforce unique value and additional benefits

**Template:**
```
[Differentiator]. [Secondary benefit]. [Trust/CTA].
```

**Example:**
```
Sports Injury And Work-Related Condition Care. Evidence-Based, Tailored To You.
```
(78 chars ✅)

## Pinning Guidelines

### What is Pinning?

Pinning locks a headline or description to a specific position.

### When to Pin

| Position | When to Pin |
|----------|-------------|
| Headline 1 | Brand name or primary keyword for consistency |
| Headline 2 | Usually don't pin - let Google optimise |
| Headline 3 | CTA headline if you want CTA always visible |
| Description 1 | Usually don't pin |
| Description 2 | Usually don't pin |

### Pinning Best Practices

**DO:**
- Pin brand/keyword to Position 1 if important for brand consistency
- Test pinned vs unpinned versions

**DON'T:**
- Over-pin (reduces Google's ability to optimise)
- Pin more than 2-3 items total
- Pin descriptions unless necessary

### Impact of Pinning

| Pinning Level | Ad Strength Impact | Recommendation |
|--------------|-------------------|----------------|
| No pins | Best Ad Strength | Let Google optimise |
| 1-2 pins | Minor impact | OK for key messages |
| 3+ pins | Significant impact | Avoid unless necessary |

## Ad Strength Indicators

### What Affects Ad Strength?

| Factor | Impact |
|--------|--------|
| Headline variety | High |
| Description variety | High |
| Keyword inclusion | Medium |
| Unique content | Medium |
| Pinning | Medium (negative) |
| Character usage | Low |

### Achieving "Excellent" Ad Strength

1. **Use all 15 headlines** - Variety is key
2. **Use all 4 descriptions** - More options = better testing
3. **Include keywords** - At least 2-3 headlines should contain keywords
4. **Vary messaging** - Don't repeat the same idea
5. **Avoid over-pinning** - Let Google optimise
6. **Use full character count** - Longer copy when valuable

## Healthcare-Specific Ad Copy Tips

### Trust Builders for Healthcare

- Years of experience
- University qualifications
- Association memberships
- Patient testimonials (with consent)

> Do not use "AHPRA registered", "specialist", or "expert" as trust builders in ad copy.

### Safe Healthcare CTAs

- "Book a consultation"
- "Schedule your assessment"
- "Call to discuss your needs"
- "Enquire about treatment options"

### Compliance-Safe Benefit Language

| Instead of | Use |
|------------|-----|
| "Cure your pain" | "Pain management solutions" |
| "Guaranteed results" | "Dedicated to your care" |
| "Best physio" | "Experienced physio team" |
| "Fast recovery" | "Supporting your recovery" |
