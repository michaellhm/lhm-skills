# Title and Headline Formulas

Proven title structures that drive clicks and rankings. All formulas target 50-60 characters for optimal SERP display.

## Numbered List Formulas

**Pattern**: [Number] [Adjective] [Topic] [Qualifier/Benefit]

Examples:
- "7 Ways to Improve Your SEO Rankings in 2024"
- "15 Content Marketing Strategies That Actually Work"
- "10 Free Tools for Keyword Research (Beginner-Friendly)"

Best practices: Odd numbers often outperform even. Use specific numbers (13 vs "over 10"). Keep under 30 for credibility.

## How-To Formulas

- How to [Goal] in [Timeframe]: "How to Rank on Google in 30 Days"
- How to [Goal] Without [Objection]: "How to Increase Traffic Without Paid Ads"
- How to [Goal] (Even If [Limitation]): "How to Rank #1 (Even If You're a New Website)"
- How to [Goal] Like [Authority]: "How to Do Keyword Research Like an SEO Pro"

## Question Formulas

- What Is [Topic]? [Hook]: "What Is GEO? Everything You Need to Know"
- Why [Action] [Result]: "Why Most Content Marketing Strategies Fail"
- Should You [Action]? [Quick Answer]: "Should You Buy Backlinks? (Spoiler: No)"

## Comparison Formulas

- [A] vs [B]: Which Is Better?: "Ahrefs vs SEMrush: Complete Comparison"
- [A] or [B] for [Goal]: "Shopify or WooCommerce for Small Business SEO"

## Ultimate Guide Formulas

- The [Complete/Definitive] Guide to [Topic]
- [Topic]: The Ultimate Guide for [Audience]
- Everything You Need to Know About [Topic]

## Power Word Categories

| Category | Words | Example |
|----------|-------|---------|
| Authority | Proven, Tested, Verified | "Proven Strategies to Double Organic Traffic" |
| Urgency | Now, Today, Quick, Fast | "Quick Wins: Improve SEO Performance Today" |
| Value | Free, Complete, Essential | "The Essential SEO Checklist" |
| Exclusivity | Secret, Hidden, Insider | "7 Little-Known SEO Tactics That Work" |
| Emotional | Simple, Easy, Powerful | "Simple SEO Strategies for Busy Entrepreneurs" |

## CTR Optimization Elements

**Increase CTR**: Year/timeframe, brackets/parentheticals, numbers, power words, target audience.

**Decrease CTR**: All caps, excessive punctuation, clickbait, misleading promises, generic titles.

## Title Length Guidelines

- **Google SERP**: 50-60 chars optimal, ~70 max before truncation
- **Mobile SERP**: ~55 chars (truncates earlier)
- **Social media**: 40-100 chars depending on platform
- **Email subject lines**: 40-50 chars optimal

## Title Writing Checklist

- [ ] Includes primary keyword naturally
- [ ] Length is 50-60 characters
- [ ] Matches search intent
- [ ] Contains power word or emotional trigger
- [ ] Specific and concrete (not vague)
- [ ] Promises clear value or benefit
- [ ] Accurate representation of content
- [ ] Works when truncated on mobile

## When to Rewrite Titles

- CTR below 2% for top 3 positions
- CTR below 5% for positions 4-10
- Content is updated (add [Updated 2024])
- Competitors have better titles
- Ranking but not getting clicks
