# Australian GBP Categories

Google Business Profile uses a country-specific category list. For Australian businesses, **only ever recommend categories that exist in the Google AU category set**. US category names are a common trap because most SEO sources, competitor scrapes, and AI training data default to American English. If you suggest a US-only category, it will not exist in the AU dropdown and the client cannot select it.

## Rules

1. **Recommend AU category names only.** Never suggest a US-only label.
2. **When unsure whether a category exists in AU**, search the GBP category picker or a current AU category source before recommending it. Do not invent or assume.
3. **If competitor research surfaces a US category name**, translate it to its AU equivalent before recommending (see mapping below).
4. **Match the exact AU spelling and wording** Google uses (e.g. "Medical Centre" not "Medical Center", "Physiotherapist" not "Physical Therapist").

## Spelling note

Australian GBP categories use British/Australian English spelling: **Centre** (not Center), **-ise** endings, etc. When in doubt, match the spelling Google shows in the AU category picker.
